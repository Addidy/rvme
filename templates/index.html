About

RV Me is bringing remote viewing target practice to the 21st century. Created by the mods of r/remoteviewing, we are aiming to solve common issues with today's target pools.

  • Never get the same target twice
  • No broken links or missing photos
  • Additional feedback
  • Endless targets
